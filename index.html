Our Story

Where craft
meets the clinic

ORA was born from a simple observation: the people who spend their lives caring for others are so often handed the least considered clothing to do it in.

Every piece is designed with intention — cut to move with you through the longest shifts, finished in fabrics that hold their shape and their colour, for professionals who deserve better than an afterthought.

From the dental chair to the treatment room, ORA is built for professionals who understand that how you show up is part of the care you give.
